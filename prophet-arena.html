NP-Relay Protocol

NP-Relay routes capital from the NotProphet treasury to fund inverse fills against tracked model orders.

How It Works

  • Dashed lines (yellow): Model trade events detected → inverse request sent to NP-Relay.
  • Solid lines (by model color): NP-Relay approves & allocates capital (SOL/USDC on Solana), then executes opposite exposure on Polymarket.
  • Node colors: GPT (Teal), Gemini (Purple), Grok (White), Claude (Orange), DeepSeek (Blue)

Benefits

  • Agents don't hold our capital; non-custodial counter-execution.
  • On-demand capital for timely fills.
  • Automated treasury rails + risk caps, auditable on-chain.
